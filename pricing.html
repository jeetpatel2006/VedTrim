Pricing FAQ

What does "1st Month Free" mean? ▼

When you sign up, your first month of consultation and care team access is completely free. You won't be charged until your second month begins. This applies to both Basic and Premium plans.

Are medicine costs included? ▼

No, medicine prices are billed separately. Your plan covers Vaidya consultations, care team access, and program management. The prescribed Ayurvedic medicines are charged based on your personalised formulation, typically ranging from ₹500-₹2,000/month.

Can I cancel anytime? ▼

Absolutely! You can cancel your plan at any time with no cancellation fees. There are no long-term contracts or commitments. Simply contact our support team at support@vedtrim.com or call +91 98790 65567.

Can I switch plans? ▼

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes will take effect from your next billing cycle. Contact our support team to make the switch.
