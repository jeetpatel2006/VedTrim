Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Ayurvedic weight loss? ▼

Ayurvedic weight loss (Medohar Chikitsa) is a holistic approach that addresses the root cause of weight gain through personalised herbal formulations, dietary corrections based on your Prakriti (body constitution), and lifestyle modifications (Dinacharya). Unlike crash diets, it promotes sustainable, healthy weight loss without side effects.

How quickly will I see results? ▼

Most members start noticing improvements in energy and digestion within 2-3 weeks. Visible weight loss typically begins within 4-6 weeks. Ayurvedic treatment focuses on sustainable, long-term results rather than quick fixes, ensuring the weight stays off.

Is VedTrim safe if I have other health conditions? ▼

Yes! Our detailed health quiz and Vaidya consultation ensure your treatment is safe and compatible with any existing conditions. Our Vaidyas review your complete health history, current medications, and conditions before prescribing any formulation. All herbs used are clinically tested and AYUSH approved.

Can I cancel my plan anytime? ▼

Absolutely! All VedTrim plans can be cancelled at any time with no cancellation fees. Your first month is free, and you're never locked into a long-term commitment. We believe in earning your trust through results, not contracts.
