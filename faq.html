Everything you need to know about VedTrim's Ayurvedic weight loss program

🌿 General Questions

What is VedTrim? ▼

VedTrim is India's premier Ayurvedic weight loss platform. We connect you with expert Vaidyas (Ayurvedic practitioners) who create personalised herbal treatment plans based on your unique body constitution (Prakriti). All consultations are online, and medicines are delivered to your doorstep.

How does Ayurvedic weight loss work? ▼

Ayurvedic weight loss (Medohar Chikitsa) works by addressing the root cause of weight gain — imbalanced Agni (digestive fire), accumulated Ama (toxins), and Prakriti imbalance. Our Vaidyas prescribe personalised herbs like Triphala, Guggulu, and Ashwagandha along with dietary and lifestyle recommendations tailored to your body type.

Is VedTrim safe? ▼

Yes! All our treatments use 100% natural, AYUSH Ministry approved herbs from GMP-certified manufacturing facilities. Every formulation is personalised after a thorough health assessment. Our Vaidyas review your complete medical history and current medications to ensure safety.

How quickly will I see results? ▼

Most members notice improved energy and digestion within 2-3 weeks. Visible weight loss typically begins within 4-6 weeks. Unlike crash diets, Ayurvedic treatment focuses on sustainable results — typically 2-4 kg per month of healthy, lasting weight loss.

Do I need a prescription? ▼

No external prescription is needed. Our qualified Vaidyas (BAMS/MD Ayurveda) assess your health through the online quiz and video consultation, then prescribe the appropriate herbal formulation for you.

🧪 Treatment & Medicines

What herbs/medicines do you use? ▼

Our formulations include classical Ayurvedic medicines such as Triphala, Medohar Guggulu, Ashwagandha, Garcinia Cambogia (Vrikshamla), Musta, and Vidanga among others. The specific combination is customised to your Prakriti and health conditions.

Are there any side effects? ▼

Ayurvedic medicines are generally very safe when prescribed correctly. Some people may experience mild detox symptoms initially (slight changes in digestion). Our Vaidyas monitor you closely and adjust formulations as needed.

Can I take VedTrim medicines with my current medications? ▼

Our Vaidyas review all your current medications during consultation to ensure compatibility. Please list all medications in your health quiz. In most cases, Ayurvedic medicines can be safely combined with allopathic treatments.
